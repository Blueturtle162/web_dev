Embroidery

Embroidery is a form of embellishment to fabric that has been around for hundreds of years. It is a relaxing pass time that can help you unwind after a stressful day. Embroidery can help you with your hand eye coordination and your gross motor skills. To begin you will need:

  • A needle
  • An embroidery hoop
  • Thread
  • Scissors
  • Cloth
